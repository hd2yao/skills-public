- Assisted-driving systems in China are regulatory and rollout dependent. The deck treats Tesla Autopilot/FSD and local NGP/NOP/NOA claims as product-positioning signals, not autonomy guarantees.
- Generated images intentionally avoid brand logos and are editorial assets, not official vehicle imagery.
